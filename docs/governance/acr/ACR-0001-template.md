## ACR Lifecycle
1. Submit ACR in `docs/governance/acr/`.
2. Review against the Architecture Constitution.
3. Evaluate business and technical impact.
4. Document alternatives and risks.
5. Approve or reject.
6. If approved, implement through ADR or governance update.
7. Archive completed ACRs with final status.
